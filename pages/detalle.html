Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO)

Release Date: August 21, 2012
Genres: First-person shooter, Action

Overview:

In Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO), players engage in intense tactical battles as either terrorists or counter-terrorists. With a variety of maps and game modes, including classic bomb defusal and hostage rescue scenarios, CS:GO offers thrilling multiplayer experiences. Teams must strategize, communicate, and coordinate their efforts to outwit their opponents. With its competitive gameplay and dedicated community, CS:GO has become a cornerstone of the esports scene.
